Überlingen - Germany

I had the pleasure of spending 7 weeks in a busy seaside resort town called Überlingen down by the beautiful lake Konstanz or as it’s known in German “Bodensee”. It’s fair to say the Germans have it nice down there during this time of the year. If you were dropped there without knowing you might think you where somewhere in the French Riviera as opposed to southern Germany. It’s warm climate and sunny summer days (on the most part) …. why so many Europeans choose to holiday there.
